My Holy Week Vacation

My family and I were traveling to a different planet when we chose to take a vacation during Holy Week. just kidding!. The reality was far more straightforward: we were traveling to our home province of Bulacan in order to pay tribute to our late grandparents and reunite with our extended family.

We set off on a road trip to our family's homeland, full of excitement and anticipation. As we traveled, we exchanged laughs, stories, and snacks, making priceless memories that would last long after the trip was over.

We were greeted with open arms by family members in Bulacan who were happy to catch up and celebrate the holiday together. Warm chats, delectable home-cooked meals, and the renewal of family ties all occurred during our vacation.

Our journey was primarily intended as a means of honoring our deceased grandparents. As a show of affection and remembrance, we went to their final resting place and brought flowers and prayers. The event served as a moving reminder of the value of family and the ongoing impact of our ancestors.
